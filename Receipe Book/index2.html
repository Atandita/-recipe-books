Ingredients

  1. Bread
  2. Butter
  3. Spices and seasoning
  4. Chaat masala
  5. Cheese

Directions

Step-1

Wash and peel a medium potato,slice to 1/4 inch thick slices.Boil them in a pan until cooked through.Drain water and set aside.

Step-2

Butter and toast the bread slices until crisp in a pan or in a toaster,ait fryer or oven.

Step-3

Add a tsp of oil and roast potatoes on both sides till crispy.You can sprinkle some seaoning if you like.set these aside.

Step-4

Add another tbsp of oil to the pan.Add garlic and saute for a minute.

Step-5

Add onions,cabage,carrots and capsium.Stir fry on a high flame for 3 to 4 mins,until slightly tender yet crunchy.Add salt,chili flakes and black pepper.Mix and set aside to cool a bit.

Step-6

Spread mayo or cream or greek yogurt as required.

Step-7

On the fiest slice place lettuce,potatoes over it,sprinkle some pepper and salt if not used eariler.Then tomatoes.

Step-8

On the slice,place lettuce and then some stir fried veggies.

Step-9

Begin to assemble.Place the bread with tomato on the bottom.

Step-10

Then place the slice with stir fried veggie over the first one.

step-11

Cover with the last slice with the mayo side down.Safe the sandwich with sandwich picks.Press down the sandwich and cut to halve or quarters.
